Method & Reliability

Data Source: All locations are manually curated and stored in places.json.

Geocoding: Addresses include latitude & longitude for distance calculations.

Distance: Computed using the Haversine formula from clinic to destination.

Walking Time: Estimated at 80 meters per minute.

Filters: Locations can be narrowed by category and tags.

Disclaimer: Always check hours and conditions before visiting.
